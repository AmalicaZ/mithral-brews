Modular Scene Building

Modular design isn’t just for websites—it’s a game-changer for tabletop storytelling. Here’s how to build reusable scenes:

  • 🧩 Use scene templates with flexible lighting and grid settings
  • 🎭 Layer in ambient sounds and journal pins
  • 🔁 Save and reuse layouts across multiple sessions

Modularity keeps your prep lean and your players immersed. Build once, adapt forever.
